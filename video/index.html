Warframe - Drums of War

About

When releasing Prime Access, a special bundle including a new playable character and weapons, Digital Extremes used to debut trailers featuring the new content in action. They haven't made new ones in a while so I thought I'd comprise a cool trailer for the game using clips from these trailers. My main focus for this project was timing everything with the music and the beat of the drums in the background, an audio track that was commonly used for various Warframe advertisements.
